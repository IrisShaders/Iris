Why Iris?

This is what makes iris different

Great mod Compatibility

Iris has little to no known compatibility issues with other mods, and any compatibility issues that exist are actively being managed.

Fabric Modding Platform

Iris uses the FabricMC Modding platform, which is an elegant and modern modloader designed with the future of minecraft modding in mind.

free and Open Source

Unlike previous shader mods, Iris is Open Source: anyone can contribute to the mod. This produces a better mod by allowing more collaboration.

Stunningly Crisp Shadows

Iris uses a technique called Shadow Frustum Culling to decrease the overhead of one of the biggest performance hogs of a shader - the shadows. Using Iris's shadow frustum culling, you'll be able to reach higher frame rates without sacrificing the quality of volumetric lighting or shadows.

Silky Smooth Entities

Iris implements a brilliant technique of rendering entities called Full Buffered Entity Rendering. This allows Minecraft objects like items, mobs, chests, and redstone to be rendered much more efficiently, increasing render speed for both the visible render and the shadow pass. This trick even works with shaders disabled, and in worlds with a lot of entities, it can boost performance up to 75 percent!
